What happens under the hood?

This demo is frontend‑only. Your real app will wire up the buttons to actual APIs.

  • 🎙️ STT — Stream mic audio → 11Labs for high‑fidelity transcription.
  • 🧭 Router — Detect language (EN/HI/Hinglish) & intent.
  • 📚 RAG — Retrieve from college knowledge base; craft a crisp answer.
  • 😏 Fallback — If out‑of‑scope: short, witty roast, stays in language.
  • 🗣️ TTS — 11Labs voice cloning renders the answer in your director’s voice.

FAQ

Does it really roast me?

Lightly! Think senior‑style leg‑pulling—never rude, always useful, and only when you go off topic.

Which languages are supported?

English, Hindi, and Hinglish. Detection is automatic, switching mid‑question works too.

Will it work in noisy spaces?

Yes. 11Labs STT is robust to campus noise. Use your phone mic or a basic earbud mic.

How do I connect my college data?

Point the backend at your knowledge sources (docs, PDFs, portals). We embed metadata and keep it fresh.
